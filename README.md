# The-Most-Epicest-Repo-you-have-ever-seen-in-your-whole-entire-life
In a world where happiness has been fractured into chaos…

Chuck Norris realizes even he is not immune to the struggles of life.
To restore balance, he goes down a journey of self-happiness using the PERMA model.
But these forces are under attack; dark entities have taken physical form.
Chuck must defeat them with both his strength and growth to find his self-happiness
